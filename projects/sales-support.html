New Engagement Sales Support

Strategic marketing content and proposals to win enterprise clients

Client: CGI Inc, Sales Support Team Year: 2021-2023 Status: 🔒 Confidential / NDA

Note: This work contains confidential client and business development information. Details below are limited to publicly shareable information. For a deeper discussion, please reach out directly.

Overview

As a consulting company, CGI was always in pursuit of new clients, and my skills as a content designer played a pivotal role in showcasing our company's services and value to prospective clients.

I created presentation decks with a strong focus on visual design, clear content, and compelling narrative—all while adhering to CGI's branding guidelines. These materials were used in high-stakes business development meetings and proposal submissions to win enterprise contracts.

My Role

  • Designed and wrote marketing content for new business proposals
  • Created presentation decks for client pitches and sales meetings
  • Developed leave-behind documentation that reinforced key value propositions
  • Collaborated closely with sales support team and account managers to refine messaging
  • Ensured content was tailored to specific client needs and industry contexts
  • Maintained brand consistency across all sales materials

Key Deliverables

Marketing Content

Strategic messaging for service offerings and capabilities

Proposal Presentations

Pitch decks for new client engagements

Leave-Behind Documentation

Reference materials reinforcing CGI's value proposition

Approach & Process

Understanding the Client

Each proposal required deep understanding of the prospective client's:

  • Industry challenges and pain points
  • Current technology landscape
  • Business objectives and strategic goals
  • Decision-maker priorities and concerns

Crafting the Narrative

I worked with the sales team to develop compelling narratives that:

  • Led with client needs, not CGI capabilities
  • Demonstrated deep understanding of their specific challenges
  • Positioned CGI's solutions as the natural fit
  • Included relevant case studies and proof points
  • Maintained a balance between technical depth and executive-level clarity

Visual Design & Brand Consistency

All materials adhered to CGI's brand guidelines while:

  • Creating visual hierarchy that guided readers through key points
  • Using data visualization to make complex information accessible
  • Ensuring consistency across all touchpoints (presentations, documents, one-pagers)
  • Adapting layouts for different audiences (technical vs. executive)

Collaboration & Iteration

This work required close partnership with:

  • Sales Support Team: Understanding client context, competitive landscape, and win themes
  • Account Managers: Refining messaging based on client conversations and feedback
  • Subject Matter Experts: Ensuring technical accuracy while maintaining accessibility
  • Leadership: Aligning on strategic positioning and key differentiators

I often went through multiple rounds of iteration, incorporating feedback from stakeholders while advocating for clear, compelling content that would resonate with decision-makers.

Skills Applied

Marketing Writing Sales Enablement Presentation Design Visual Design Brand Management Storytelling Stakeholder Management B2B Marketing

Key Learnings

  • Lead with the client's story: The most effective proposals started with the client's challenges, not CGI's capabilities
  • Executive vs. technical audiences require different approaches: Learned to create layered content that served both simultaneously
  • Visual design amplifies messaging: Strong visual hierarchy and data visualization made complex proposals more persuasive
  • Iteration is essential: Best results came from multiple rounds of refinement with stakeholder input
  • Consistency builds trust: Maintaining brand consistency across all materials reinforced professionalism and reliability
